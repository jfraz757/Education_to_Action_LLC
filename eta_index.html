Putting Theory Into Practice For A Better Tomorrow

At Education to Action LLC, we use a combination of research and community organizing experience to inform, develop, and execute initiatives aimed at addressing the many different disparities that impact both contemporary and historically marginalized groups. We will proudly work in community alongside any institution, organization, or individual that wants to utilize pragmatic, data-informed, and needs-based approaches in order to create equitable and just communities for all.

Economic Equity

The People's Ledger

A public directory of minority and underrepresented businesses in Kentucky — rebuilt as an act of resistance after coordinated legal attacks on economic equity programs forced the original records dark. Over 1,100 certified businesses. Free to search, free to submit.

Workforce Transparency

CandidateVoice.org

An anonymous candidate experience review platform exposing hiring process transparency — or the lack of it — across 400+ companies. Job seekers share what actually happened: ghosted, rejected, hired, or still waiting. Every review is a data point. Every data point is a pattern.

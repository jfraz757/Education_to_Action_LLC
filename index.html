Putting Theory Into Practice For A Better Tomorrow

At Education to Action LLC, we use a combination of research and community organizing experience to inform, develop, and execute initiatives aimed at addressing the many different disparities that impact both contemporary and historically marginalized groups. We will proudly work in community alongside any institution, organization, or individual that wants to utilize pragmatic, data-informed, and needs-based approaches in order to create equitable and just communities for all.
